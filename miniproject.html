ABOUT LIONEL MESSI :

Lionel Andres Messi (“Lionel Messi”) is one of the most popular soccer player in the world. Born on June 24 1987
Messi was the youngest player ever to play for FC Barcelona. At the age of 16, Messi made his first appearance for FC Barcelona
Messi was born in Rosario, Argentina and moved to Spain at the age of 13 where he was offered the chance to train at FC Barcelona.
