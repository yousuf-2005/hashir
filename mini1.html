Lionel Messi's Achievements

Major Achievements

  • 10-time La Liga winner with FC Barcelona
  • 4-time UEFA Champions League winner with FC Barcelona
  • 6-time Ballon d'Or winner (as of 2022)
  • Argentina national team captain and 2021 Copa America winner
  • Most goals scored in a calendar year (91 goals in 2012)
  • Most goals scored for a single club (672 goals for FC Barcelona)
  • Most assists in La Liga history
  • Most hat-tricks in La Liga history
  • Most goals scored in a single La Liga season (50 goals in 2011-2012)
  • Only player to win the European Golden Shoe 5 times
